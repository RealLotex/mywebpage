What can I do you?

Mobile optimized stylized models

Considering the highest requirements of optimization, I can model objects, structures and characters, achieving a good topology and a stylized look made by texture painting that will make use of "Draw Call Batching" in Unity3D.

Sculpting and Retopologizing

For the need of detailed characters and environments, I can sculpt the most complex designs, and then retopologize them, obtaining meshes with a variety of level of details (LOD) that will stay simple and get complex when needed.

Rigging, Animating and Unity Exporting

I can rig humanoids and quadrupeds, animate them and generate a .unitypackage that contains the model, it's materials, the animations and the preconfigured FBX package according to the Unity3D conventions.
